Selling Fee

When you make a sale, we deduct a fee from your payout automatically. We call this a selling fee. The selling fee is 10% per invoice paid. You keep the rest of your earnings. Here's an example of how we apply the selling fee to your sales. Let's say that you're providing multiple services:

  • If the first service is $500.00 USD, we'll deduct $50.00 for the selling fee.
  • If the second service is $100.00, we'll deduct $10.00 for the selling fee.

The selling fee:

  • Includes the cost of payment processing. (2.9% Stripe fee + Our 7.1% fee = 10% Selling fee)
  • Applies to all checkout transactions for all service categories on Redbud Way.
  • Helps fund the Redbud Way experience.
